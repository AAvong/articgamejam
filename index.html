What is Arctic Game Jam?

Arctic Game Jam is a one-of-a-kind event focusing on addressing climate-related challenges. The task is to design a game and the possibles contexts are greenhouses gas emissions, water harvesting, low carbon solutions, energy management and consumption, extreme weather, sustainable agriculture.

From Wednesday November 20th till Friday 22th, participants all over the world will join, work as a team, and each team will design a game.


The initiative was jointly conceived by Green Gaming Project and partners
It is supported/sponsored by Luleå University of Technology, and European Union,

Venue: Online via zoom, on this link

Come, join us and register here!

F.A.Q.

F.A.Q.

Can I attend?

Anyone who has the competence to attend is welcome.

Challenges

The challenges will be presented later.

Awards and criteria

The best contributions will get awards. Mostly this will be symbolic awards like diplomas.

Tools

You bring your own laptop to work on.

Intellectual properties

Everything developed at the hackathon will be open source.

What will I eat?

We’ll provide food for all meals.
If you have dietary restrictions, we’ll try our best to accommodate them.
Of course, we’ll also have an abundance of snacks and refreshments throughout the hacking period.

What should I bring?

Bring a laptop for hacking.

How do teams work?

Teams are at most 5 people. You can select teammates before you register, or you can register individually without specified teammates.
It will be possible to find teammates when the hackathon starts.

What if I don’t have a team?

If you don’t have a team now, that’s okay! We’ll help to form teams when the hackathon starts.

Does this cost money?

Nope! Admission is free and includes meals, snacks and a memorable experience (if we do say so ourselves)!

What if this FAQ didn’t answer my questions?

Please email us at marie.nolin@associated.ltu.se if you’re in any way confused or concerned! We’d love to help you out :)
